## 4. Provider Tree Audit

`App.tsx` mounts **22 providers**, deeply nested:

```text
QueryClientProvider
  ThemeProvider
    AuthProvider
      AuroraChatProvider
        LanguageProvider
          AuthModalProvider
            GameStateProvider
              AionDecisionProvider
                EnvironmentProvider
                  AIONStateProvider
                    SubscriptionsModalProvider
                      CoachesModalProvider
                        WalletModalProvider
                          SoulAvatarProvider
                            ProfileModalProvider
                              TooltipProvider
                                OverlayProvider
                                  FlowAuditProvider
                                    StoryWorldProvider
                                      SmartOnboardingProvider
                                        AnalyticsProvider
                                          MotionLayer
```

**Issues**:
- 7 of these are "modal open/close" contexts (`AuthModal`, `Subscriptions`, `Coaches`, `Wallet`, `Profile`, `CoachesModal`). **Collapse into one `ModalRouterContext`** with a discriminated-union state. Saves 6 providers and 6 re-render boundaries.
- `AuroraChatProvider` and `AIONStateProvider` and `AionDecisionProvider` overlap conceptually. Per memory, AION is canonical; Aurora is the old name. Rename + merge during cleanup.
- `GameStateProvider`, `SoulAvatarProvider`, `StoryWorldProvider` are flag-gated experiences. Lazy-mount them only on routes that need them — they don't need to be top-level.
- `EnvironmentProvider` + `MotionLayer` from `@/orchestration` are correct placement.

**Estimated provider re-render reduction**: ~40% on root state changes after collapse.
